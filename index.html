Guess the Color

A simple app used to train your knowledge of HTML color hex codes.

Quick, how would you describe #2dd687? Let's see... it's got a lot of green, a bit of blue, and not a lot of red. Is it more like this? Or maybe more like this?

With Guess the Color, now you can train your brain to learn these differences and get more readily accessible and more accurate hex code knowledge. Try it out today — it's available for free on the iOS App Store.
